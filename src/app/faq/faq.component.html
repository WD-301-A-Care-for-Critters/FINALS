Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

What can I do to help animals in my everyday life?

Adopt, Don't Shop: If you're considering adding a pet to your family, adopt from a shelter or rescue organization rather than buying from a breeder or pet store. By adopting, you're giving a home to an animal in need and helping to reduce pet overpopulation.

Support Animal Welfare Organizations: Donate your time, money, or resources to local animal shelters, rescue groups, or advocacy organizations. You can volunteer at a shelter, foster animals in need, or participate in fundraising events to support their efforts.

Reduce Your Environmental Footprint: Make environmentally-friendly choices that benefit animals and their habitats, such as reducing single-use plastics, conserving water and energy, and supporting sustainable agriculture practices.

How can I support A Care for Critters?

By Donating: Research and identify reputable animal welfare organizations, such as local animal shelters, rescue groups, wildlife conservation organizations, and advocacy groups.

Sponsor an Animal: Many animal shelters and rescue organizations offer sponsorship programs where you can financially support the care of a specific animal. Your sponsorship can help cover expenses such as food, medical care, and shelter for an animal in need.

Support Animal Sanctuaries: Animal sanctuaries provide lifelong care and refuge to animals rescued from cruelty, neglect, and exploitation. Consider donating to sanctuaries that provide a safe and nurturing environment for rescued farm animals, exotic animals, or wildlife.
